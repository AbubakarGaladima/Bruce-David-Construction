President

Bruce began his professional career in the United States Marine Corps where he served on Force Reconnaissance team. He later began his career in construction by working as a NCDOT Inspector, Surveyor, Project Engineer, Estimator, Regional Manager and eventually Vice President.

When he is not working, Bruce enjoys spending time with his family at the lake, attending baseball games with his son, horse-riding lessons and gymnastics with his daughters.

Bruce earned his Master of Business Administration with an emphasis on Strategy and Leadership from The University of Manchester UK. He also qualifies and maintains a Houston Unclassified General Contractors License. He is also an active member of the Houston Association of General Contractors.

Founded in 2016, Bruce David Construction Company serves as a heavy civil contractor located in Houston. Our focus is on building and maintaining client relationships, as we help them achieve their project-specific goals. By having a multi-functional, diversified fleet of equipment and team, it allows us to cater to the needs of the individual project whether it be in public, multi-family, industrial, commercial, municipal, NCDOT, or land development sector.
