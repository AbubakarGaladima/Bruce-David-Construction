Why Choose Us?

Our Attention To Detail

We treat each project as if it is our own home. We do not cut any corners and we strive to ensure all aspects of the build, even the parts you do not see, are carried with our same meticulous attention to detail. Our team are made up of talented, highly skilled trades people all of whom have industry leading qualifications in their respective fields. This includes Gas Safe, NiCEIC, Cure It and we are proud members of The Federation of Master Builders (FMB).

Always avaliable

Finding a builder can be a very risky business. When you employ Hawksmoor, you completely remove this risk and our countless successful projects will attest to that. We love to show our potential new clients around our ongoing and our finished projects as we leave every project on great terms with each of our clients. We promise you that we will exceed your expectations of what a builder can be.

Professional and responsible

It is inevitable that some issues will arise on site, but we pride ourselves on handling them in a fast, responsive fashion to find the solution and minimise the stress that they may potentially cause you. Equally if any issues arise after the project has finished, our aftercare service is second to none.
